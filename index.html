We help children and young people to overcome the personal challenges they face in life.


Our Mission


Families First offer services to children aged 5-16 years, living in north east Fife. We follow guidance from Getting it Right for Every Child (GIRFEC) and the well-being indicators within the guidance.

Consistent with the joint approach that Fife is taking we want the children and young people we work with to be: Confident individuals, effective contributors, successful learners and responsible citizens.

What we offer


We offer 3 core services to families living in north east Fife. These are the One-to-One Befriending Service, the Children’s Group Work Service and the Family Support Service.

We developed these services as a direct result of consulting with and listening to the children, families and partner agencies we work with.

Get Involved


We have a strong commitment to the volunteers who work with us. Families First volunteers hold a number of roles within the organisation which include Trustees, administration, driving, and direct service delivery.

With your help, we are able to deliver the services we commit to and ensure that our service users receive a quality service that meets and supports their needs.
