Aims

  • Mission: FFSA will work alongside children and their families to overcome the personal challenges they face in life.
  • Scope: To work with families who have children in the household aged 5-16 years of age with additional support needs living in North East Fife.

Outcomes

  • A reduction in isolation
  • A reduction in stress and difficulties
  • An increase in confidence and self-esteem
  • An improvement in relationships

Objectives

  • One-to One Befriending Service, to 60 children per annum
  • Family Support Service, to 60 adults per annum (9 high, 20 medium, 31 low intensity)
  • Children's Group work Service, to 50 children per annum
