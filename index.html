We help children and young people to overcome the personal challenges they face in life.



Families First offer services to children aged 5-16 years, living in north east Fife. We follow guidance from Getting it Right for Every Child (GIRFEC) and the well-being indicators within the guidance.

Consistent with the joint approach that Fife is taking we want the children and young people we work with to be: Confident individuals, effective contributors, successful learners and responsible citizens.

Our 3 core services are the One-to-One Befriending Service, the Children’s Group Work Service and the Family Support Service. We developed these services as a direct result of consulting with and listening to the children, families and partner agencies.
